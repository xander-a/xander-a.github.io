My name is Xander D. Alfaro . I am from Sta. Mesa Manila and currently working as computer engineer in Polytechnic University of the Philippines- Institute of Technology.

As a person who is involved in computer, I believe that I can achieve something through the knowledge I have.

I found my own company to help people in need of materials either for gaming or personal use. This is also to inspire and encourage young people to be interested in computer hardwares.

As you know, Philippines has been lacking on technologies for the past decades and I believe that having a business that primarily focus on computer hardwares will have a huge impact to our small tech society.

I believe that someday, we will have our chance to compete with leading big tech companies abroad.

This company was founded on August of 2022 by Engineer Xander Dela Cruz Alfaro. He is the current CEO working and operating this company together with his co-founder Mr. John Emmerson B. Cuña.
